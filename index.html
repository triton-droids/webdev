A Future Worth Building

Our Challenge

At Triton Droids, we're taking on one of the biggest challenges of our time: creating affordable, high-efficiency humanoid robots that can level the playing field in global production.

Our Mission

To design robots that can work in any environment, from dangerous mines to remote farms, and empower communities that have been left behind by technology.

Our Focus

Bridging the tech gap between the rich and poor countries can make an incremental difference in value creation by over $2 trillion.

How We Aim to Achieve Our Mission

Leveraging UCSD's Unique Assets

  • Expert Faculty Collaboration: Access to multidisciplinary experts ensures holistic, socially impactful robot development.
  • Student Volunteer Network: Large student body aids in data collection, user testing, and real-world development.
  • Advanced Data Infrastructure: UCSD data center enables complex simulations and AI development.
